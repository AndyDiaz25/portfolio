Pirates of the Caribbean: The Curse of the Black Pearl

Rating: PG-13
Release date: July 9, 2003
Genre: Action-Adventure, Fantasy
Directed by: Gore Verbinski
Written by: Ted Elliot, Terry Rossio, Stuart Beatie

A thrilling high-seas adventure with a mysterious twist that started it all. The roughish yet charming Captain Jack Sparrow’s idyllic pirate life capsizes after his nemesis, the wily Captain Barbossa steals his ship, the Black Pearl, and later attacks the town of Port Royal—kidnapping the Governor’s beautiful daughter, Elizabeth. In a gallant attempt to rescue her and recapture the Black Pearl, Elizabeth’s childhood friend Will Turner joins forces with Jack. What Will does not know is that a cursed treasure has doomed Barbossa and his crew to live forever as the undead.
